What we do

We made it our job, to offer a All-in-one page for people on the search for helping animals in need. Wether you are only looking to place a donation, take over a sponsorship or even adopt an animal from a shelter, you will find anything! Also all Sanctuarys, Shelters and Zoos can keep the community up-to-date via the photo gallary on our page. All the newest animals that have arrived, have found their home or meet their sponsor can be shared there! Our news section will be filled with all the news from the said places, or from news from our team! Have a look there too!

Donation

With your Donation, you help us to ensure that the animals in our care receive species-appropriate housing, care and attention for the rest of their lives. You can choose the destination of your donation from all the locations that are in partnership with us! You can even place a Donation as a gift to someone else.
